About Us

Established in 2009, Sunrise Edu Hub in Nechhwa, Sikar, stands as a beacon of educational excellence and innovation. Our mission is to transform rural societies by imparting value-added education to the children of the region and beyond.

As an English medium co-educational institution, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive educational opportunities, from school to degree college, including specialized courses in pharmacy, Sainik Military preparation, IIT coaching, and NEET coaching.

At Sunrise Edu Hub, we believe in creating a climate conducive to learning, where both teachers and students eagerly anticipate their return after holidays. Our commitment to quality education and holistic development makes us the best educational hub in Sikar.
